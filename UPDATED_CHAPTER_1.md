**CHAPTER 1**

**BACKGROUND OF THE STUDY**

**Introduction**

In the modern educational landscape, the efficiency of administrative processes is crucial to the success of an institution and the satisfaction of its students. Yllana Bay View College, like many growing educational institutions, faces the challenge of managing a large volume of student data, subject offerings, and academic records.

Currently, many processes such as subject searching, grade tracking, attendance monitoring, and permit issuance are handled through semi-manual or fragmented digital systems. This often leads to delays in enrollment, difficulty in tracking student progress, and potential errors in academic reporting. Students frequently experience long wait times for simple transactions, while administrative staff spend excessive time searching through physical ledgers or disconnected spreadsheets to verify records.

To address these challenges, the proponents developed the Student Subject Management & Tracing System. This system is designed to centralize academic data, automate grade recording and attendance, and streamline the issuance of student permits and tuition monitoring. By transitioning to a unified web-based platform, the college can ensure faster, more accurate, and more reliable services for its entire academic community.

**Project Context**

The existing manual and semi-digital methods serve as a significant bottleneck to institutional efficiency. The proposed Student Subject Management & Tracing System aims to eliminate these drawbacks by providing a real-time, centralized database for all student-related transactions.

This study will assist the organization in providing an accurate and satisfying experience for students and staff alike. The system will allow for easy tracking of standardized subject schedules, academic performance, and semester-based financial balances. If implemented, it will drastically reduce paper usage, minimize the risk of data loss, and allow the administration to generate detailed reports—such as printable student ledgers—in seconds rather than hours.

**Purpose & Description of the Study**

The Student Subject Management & Tracing System provides a secure and reliable platform to handle core academic operations. It replaces time-consuming manual lookups with a fast, search-optimized interface secured by dynamic granular permissions.

**Beneficiaries of the system include:**

*   **Students:** Can easily view their own grades, subject schedules, attendance, ledger records, and permit status without visiting multiple offices.
*   **Teachers:** Can efficiently manage student lists, input grades, and track daily attendance automatically based on enrolled subjects.
*   **Registrar:** Gains a centralized tool to manage subject offerings, standardized schedules, and oversee academic records.
*   **SAPS (Student Affairs/Student Programs):** Can easily assign and manage permit numbers.
*   **Cashier:** Can record payments, monitor semester-based student balances with accuracy, and generate ledger booklets.
*   **Administration/Owner:** Can monitor overall institutional trends, configure granular access roles for staff, and ensure data integrity.

**Significance Of The Study**

The Student Subject Management & Tracing System holds significant value as it addresses the long-standing challenges of manual academic record-keeping. The successful implementation of this study will benefit the following:

*   **To the Students:** It provides them with a faster and more transparent way to view their grades, financial ledgers, and secure their exam permits. This reduces the frustration of waiting in long queues and ensures they stay informed about their academic status.
*   **To the Teachers:** It simplifies the complex task of grade computation, attendance tracking, and management. Teachers can focus more on student engagement rather than spending hours on manual data entry and spreadsheet calculations.
*   **To the Registrar's Office:** It streamlines subject management and enrollment monitoring. The system minimizes human error in recording subjects and standardized schedules, ensuring that the college's data remains accurate and reliable.
*   **To the SAPS and Cashier Offices:** It provides an efficient way to track student permits and tuition balances per semester, reducing administrative workload and improving financial record-keeping through automated ledger printing.
*   **To the School Administration:** It enhances the overall reputation of Yllana Bay View College by professionalizing its student services and administrative operations through modern technology and secure role-based access.
*   **To Future Researchers:** This study will serve as a valuable reference and guide for other IT students conducting research on student management systems or similar educational technologies.

**Objectives**

The primary objective of this study is to develop a Student Subject Management & Tracing System for Yllana Bay View College to automate academic recording and improve the efficiency of student services through modern technology.

**Specific Objectives:**

1.  To create a centralized database for student profiles, subject offerings, grade records, and automatic attendance tracking.
2.  To implement a granular role-based access control (RBAC) system to ensure dynamic data security and privacy across different departments.
3.  To develop an automated process for generating student permits, tracking semester-based tuition balances, and printing official student ledger booklets.
4.  To build a dynamic Class Attendance Management system featuring spreadsheet-style tracking, visual summaries, and automated Excel (.xlsx) exportation.
5.  To provide a user-friendly interface with standardized scheduling options (e.g., MWF, TTHS, Saturday) for searching subjects and viewing academic performance.
6.  To evaluate the system's performance in terms of accuracy, reliability, and user satisfaction.

**Scope and Limitation**

**This section outlines what the system can do and what is beyond its current capability.**

**Scope**

*   **User Management:** Secure login with Granular Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) for Students, Teachers, Registrar, Cashier, SAPS, and Admin, allowing specific permissions per module.
*   **Subject Tracking:** Searching and managing standardized subject schedules (MWF, TTHS, Saturday), rooms, and professors.
*   **Grade Management:** Inputting, computing, and viewing student grades (Prelim to Final).
*   **Attendance Management:** Teacher-centric tracking interface with auto-enrollment based on subjects, daily status monitoring, and automated Excel/CSV spreadsheet report generation.
*   **Permit System:** Issuing and monitoring student permit numbers for exams.
*   **Student Ledger & Payments:** Tracking semester-based tuition and custom fees, printing physical POS receipts, and generating full ledger booklets.

**Limitation**

*   **No Online Payments:** All payments must still be settled over-the-counter at the Cashier’s office.
*   **Web-Only:** The system is accessed through a web browser and does not have a dedicated mobile app (iOS/Android).
*   **Local Network:** The system requires an active internet connection or a local server connection to function.
*   **No Automated SMS:** The system does not send automatic text messages for dues, attendance alerts, or grade updates.

**Definition Of Terms**

*   **SMS (Student Management System):** The software application used to manage student data and academic processes.
*   **SAPS:** Student Affairs/Student Programs; the office responsible for student welfare and permit issuance.
*   **Permit Number:** A unique code assigned to students allowing them to take major examinations.
*   **GPA (Grade Point Average):** The calculated average of a student's grades used to measure academic performance.
*   **Granular Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** A security method that allows specific system permissions (Create, Read, Update, Delete) to be individually configured for each user role, restricting unauthorized access to sensitive modules.
*   **Tuition Balance:** The remaining amount a student needs to pay for their school fees for a specific semester.
*   **Student Ledger Booklet:** A generated, printable document that records a student's complete financial assessment and payment history in a professional layout.
*   **Attendance Spreadsheet:** A visual, grid-based tracking tool within the system that allows teachers to record daily attendance, view summaries, and export records directly to Excel.
